# LinkedIn First Message

## Purpose

A personalized first outreach message designed to initiate conversations with passive candidates by demonstrating genuine interest in their experience and highlighting a relevant career opportunity.

---

## Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

I came across your profile and was impressed by your work on **[Project/Technology/Domain]**. Your experience with **[specific skill, product, or achievement]** particularly caught my attention, and I felt it was worth reaching out.

I'm currently hiring for a **[Job Title]** position with **[Company Name]**, where we're looking for someone with a background very similar to yours. The role offers an opportunity to work on **[brief description of the product, platform, technology, or business initiative]**, while collaborating with **[global teams, large scale systems, cutting edge technologies, or another genuine value proposition]**.

Based on what I saw on your profile, I believe your experience could be a strong fit for what we're building.

If this opportunity sounds interesting, I'd be happy to share more about the role, the team, and what makes this opportunity unique. We can connect over a quick 10 to 15 minute call whenever it's convenient for you.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**  
Talent Acquisition

---

## Best Practices

- Personalize the first paragraph using the candidate's profile.
- Highlight a genuine value proposition of the role or company.
- Keep the message concise and conversational.
- Avoid generic copy-paste messaging.
- End with a simple, low pressure invitation to connect.

---

## Suitable For

- Passive candidates
- Technical hiring
- Leadership hiring
- Lateral hiring
