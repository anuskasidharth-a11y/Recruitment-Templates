# LinkedIn Follow-up Message

## Purpose

This template is designed to follow up with candidates who have not responded to the initial LinkedIn outreach. The objective is to politely re-engage them without sounding repetitive or pushy.

---

## Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

I wanted to follow up on my previous message regarding the **[Job Title]** opportunity with **[Company Name]**. I understand you may have had a busy schedule, so I thought I'd reach out once more.

Based on your experience in **[Technology/Skill/Domain]**, I genuinely believe your background aligns well with what we're looking for. The role offers the opportunity to contribute to **[brief project, product, or business initiative]** while working with a collaborative team and solving meaningful challenges.

If this sounds like something you'd be interested in exploring, I'd be happy to share more details and answer any questions. We can connect over a quick 10 to 15 minute conversation at a time that's convenient for you.

If now isn't the right time, I completely understand and appreciate your consideration.

Wishing you continued success, and I hope to hear from you.

Best regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

---

## Best Practices

- Wait 4 to 7 days before sending a follow-up.
- Keep the tone polite and professional.
- Add new information instead of repeating the first message.
- Respect the candidate's decision if they are not interested.
- Avoid sending multiple follow-ups without a response.

---

## Suitable For

- Passive candidates
- Technical hiring
- Leadership hiring
- Lateral hiring
