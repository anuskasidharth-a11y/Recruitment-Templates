# Cold Email Template

## Purpose

This template is designed for reaching out to potential candidates via email when LinkedIn messaging is unavailable or email is the preferred communication channel. The objective is to introduce a relevant opportunity, establish credibility, and encourage an initial conversation.

---

## Subject Line Options

- Opportunity for a **[Job Title]** at **[Company Name]**
- Your experience in **[Technology/Domain]** caught my attention
- Exploring a **[Job Title]** opportunity with **[Company Name]**
- Thought this opportunity might interest you

---

## Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

I came across your profile while searching for professionals with experience in **[Technology/Skill/Domain]**, and I was particularly impressed by your work on **[specific project, technology, certification, or achievement]**.

I'm currently hiring for a **[Job Title]** position with **[Company Name]**. Based on your background, I believe your experience aligns well with what we're looking for.

The role offers an opportunity to work on **[product, platform, business initiative, or transformation]**, while collaborating with talented teams and contributing to impactful projects.

If this sounds interesting, I'd be happy to share more details about the role, the team, and answer any questions you may have. We can also schedule a quick 10 to 15 minute conversation at a time that's convenient for you.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

Email: **[Recruiter Email]**

Phone: **[Recruiter Phone]**

---

## Best Practices

- Use a clear and relevant subject line.
- Personalize the email using the candidate's profile.
- Keep the email concise and easy to read.
- Highlight the opportunity instead of overselling it.
- Include clear contact information.
- End with a simple, low-pressure call to action.

---

## Suitable For

- Technical hiring
- Non-technical hiring
- Leadership hiring
- Passive candidates
