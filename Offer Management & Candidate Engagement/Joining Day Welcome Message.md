# Joining Day Welcome Message

## Purpose

This template is used to welcome new employees on their first day, provide essential joining information, and establish a positive onboarding experience.

---

## Subject

Welcome to **[Company Name]** | Wishing You Success in Your New Role

---

## Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

Welcome to **[Company Name]**.

On behalf of the Talent Acquisition team, congratulations on joining us as a **[Job Title]**. We are delighted to have you on board and wish you every success in your new role.

Your onboarding journey begins today, and our teams are committed to ensuring a smooth transition. Should you require any assistance regarding documentation, system access, reporting details, or onboarding formalities, please feel free to reach out at any time.

We hope your experience with **[Company Name]** is both professionally rewarding and personally fulfilling.

Once again, welcome to the team, and we wish you a successful journey ahead.

Kind regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

**[Company Name]**

---

## When to Use

- On the candidate's official joining date.
- After onboarding formalities have commenced.
- As part of the organization's new hire communication.

---

## Best Practices

- Send the email during the first half of the joining day.
- Include relevant onboarding contacts if applicable.
- Keep the tone warm while maintaining professionalism.
- Ensure all joining formalities have been communicated separately.
