# Offer Release Email

## Purpose

This template is used to formally inform a selected candidate that their offer has been released while clearly communicating the next steps and maintaining a positive candidate experience.

---

## Subject

Offer Letter Released | **[Job Title]** | **[Company Name]**

---

## Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

Congratulations, and thank you for your time throughout our hiring process.

We are delighted to inform you that your offer letter for the position of **[Job Title]** at **[Company Name]** has been released.

You can review the offer details, including your compensation, benefits, joining date, and employment terms, by accessing the attached document or the recruitment portal.

Please take some time to review the offer carefully. If you have any questions regarding the role, compensation, joining process, or documentation, feel free to reach out. I'll be happy to assist you.

Kindly share your decision by **[Date]** so that we can plan the next steps accordingly.

We are excited about the possibility of having you join our team and look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

**[Company Name]**

---

## Best Practices

- Verify all offer details before sending.
- Clearly communicate the response deadline.
- Encourage candidates to ask questions.
- Keep the tone warm, professional, and informative.
- Share relevant onboarding information when applicable.
