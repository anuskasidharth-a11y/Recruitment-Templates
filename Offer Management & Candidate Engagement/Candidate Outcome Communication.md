# Candidate Outcome Communication

## Purpose

This document contains professional communication templates for common candidate outcomes during the recruitment process. The objective is to ensure every candidate receives timely, respectful, and transparent communication while maintaining a positive employer brand and candidate experience.

---

# Scenario 1: Candidate Not Selected

## Subject

Update on Your Application | **[Job Title]** | **[Company Name]**

### Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

Thank you for taking the time to participate in our recruitment process for the **[Job Title]** position.

After careful consideration, we have decided to move forward with another candidate whose experience more closely aligns with the current requirements of the role.

We sincerely appreciate your interest in **[Company Name]** and the time you invested throughout the process.

We wish you continued success in your career and hope to stay connected for future opportunities that may better match your profile.

Kind regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

**[Company Name]**

# Scenario 2: Candidate Declines the Offer

## Subject

Thank You for Your Update | **[Company Name]**

### Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

Thank you for letting us know about your decision.

While we're naturally disappointed that we won't have the opportunity to work together at this time, we appreciate your transparency throughout the recruitment process.

We wish you every success in your future endeavors and hope our paths cross again should another opportunity arise.

Thank you once again, and we wish you all the very best.

Kind regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

---

# Scenario 3: Position Put on Hold

## Subject

Update Regarding Your Application | **[Company Name]**

### Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

Thank you for your continued interest in **[Company Name]**.

I wanted to inform you that the hiring process for the **[Job Title]** position has been temporarily placed on hold due to a business decision.

We appreciate your patience and will keep your profile under active consideration. As soon as we have further updates regarding the position, I'll reach out to you.

Thank you for your understanding.

Kind regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

---

## Best Practices

- Communicate outcomes promptly.
- Maintain a respectful and appreciative tone.
- Avoid providing subjective interview feedback unless company policy permits.
- Keep the communication concise and transparent.
- Leave the conversation on a positive note to strengthen the employer brand.
