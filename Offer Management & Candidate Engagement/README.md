# Offer Management & Candidate Engagement

This folder contains templates used after a candidate has successfully completed the interview process.

The objective is to maintain candidate engagement, improve the offer acceptance rate, minimize offer dropouts, and deliver a positive candidate experience until the candidate joins the organization.

## Templates

- Offer Release Email
- Offer Acceptance Follow-up
- Pre-Joining Candidate Check-in
- Joining Day Welcome Message
- Candidate Closure Communication

## Purpose

Candidate engagement does not end when an offer is released. Consistent communication throughout the pre-joining period helps build trust, address concerns, and improve the overall hiring experience while reducing the risk of offer declines and no-shows.
