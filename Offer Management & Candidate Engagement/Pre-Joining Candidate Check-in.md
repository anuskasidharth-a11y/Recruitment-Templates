# Pre-Joining Candidate Check-in

## Purpose

This template is used to maintain engagement with candidates after offer acceptance and before their joining date. It helps confirm joining readiness, address potential concerns proactively, and reduce the risk of offer dropouts.

---

## Subject

Pre-Joining Confirmation | **[Job Title]** | **[Company Name]**

---

## Email Template

Hi **[Candidate Name]**,

As your scheduled joining date approaches, I wanted to connect and ensure that everything is progressing as planned.

This is a quick touchpoint to confirm that you have received all the necessary information regarding your onboarding process and to check if there have been any changes to your joining timeline, notice period, or availability.

If you have any questions related to your offer, documentation, onboarding formalities, reporting manager, work location, or joining process, please feel free to reach out. I'll be happy to assist and ensure you have the information you need before your first day.

If everything is on track, a quick confirmation from your end would be appreciated.

We look forward to welcoming you to **[Company Name]** and wish you continued success as you prepare for this exciting transition.

Kind regards,

**[Recruiter Name]**

Talent Acquisition

**[Company Name]**

---

## When to Use

- One to two weeks before the joining date.
- During a long notice period.
- Whenever proactive candidate engagement is required before onboarding.

---

## Best Practices

- Keep the communication concise and professional.
- Confirm joining readiness without sounding intrusive.
- Encourage candidates to discuss concerns early.
- Reinforce recruiter availability for onboarding support.
- Document any risks or changes discussed during the conversation.
