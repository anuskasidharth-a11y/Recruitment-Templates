# Recruitment Templates

A practical collection of professional recruitment templates designed to support every stage of the hiring lifecycle.

This repository contains reusable templates for candidate outreach, stakeholder communication, interview management, offer management, and candidate engagement.

The goal is to demonstrate structured recruitment practices that improve communication, consistency, and candidate experience throughout the hiring process.

---

# Repository Structure

```
Recruitment Templates
│
├── Candidate Outreach
├── Hiring Manager Collaboration
├── Interview Management
└── Offer Management & Candidate Engagement
```

---

# What's Included

### Candidate Outreach
Professional communication templates for approaching and engaging candidates.

### Hiring Manager Collaboration
Templates that improve communication with hiring managers throughout the recruitment process.

### Interview Management
Structured documents for interview feedback and candidate evaluation.

### Offer Management & Candidate Engagement
Templates covering offer release, candidate follow-ups, joining communication, and professional closure messages.

---

# Purpose

This repository demonstrates practical recruitment documentation used throughout the end-to-end hiring lifecycle.
